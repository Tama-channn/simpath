Level 3: Advanced Simulation Brief

Basic Business Case Analysis

Evaluates logical problem-solving, structural analytical structures, and business optimization strategies.

⏱️ Duration: 20 minutes 📝 Format: Short Essay (150 - 250 words) 💯 Max Score: 100 pts

📊 BUSINESS CASE SCENARIO

You have just joined GreenBrew—a premium organic coffee startup operating in Ho Chi Minh City for 2 years—as a Business Analyst Intern. Your manager hands you the latest monthly operating ledger and says: "Our total top-line revenue dropped 18% compared to last month. Analyze the metrics and identify the structural issues at play."

Previous Month Revenue
320M VND
Current Month Revenue
262M VND
Order Volume
-12% Drop
Average Order Value
-7% Drop
Operating Costs
Unchanged

❓ ASSIGNMENT QUESTIONS

a) In your assessment, what are the two most probable root causes driving this top-line revenue contraction? Provide metric-backed justifications for your choices.

b) Propose exactly one tactical action item your manager can execute next week to validate your hypothesized root causes.

💡 Hint: Focus closely on the divergent patterns between shrinking order volume and compressing average transaction value—they indicate completely different underlying issues.

OFFICIAL GRADING RUBRIC

Problem Identification 30 pts

Clearly distinguishes the two drop factors; does not confuse symptoms with core root causes.

Analytical Logic 30 pts

Provides rational logic frameworks; successfully incorporates provided numerical data points.

Action Proposal 30 pts

Proposes a practical action item executable within 1 week with clear operational verification goals.

Structure & Clarity 10 pts

Answers all sub-questions cleanly; maintains a concise professional tone; meets length parameters.
